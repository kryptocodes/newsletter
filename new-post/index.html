Rotaract Club Inauguration

Published Jul 21, 2019 by Srivatsan at https://kryptocodes.github.io/newsletter/new-post/

Rotaract Club of Amrita College of Engineering and Technology was inaugurated on 3rd April, 2019. The office bearers and Board of Directors were selected and assigned roles in the inauguration. The Chief Guest, Rtn. DGND.Major Donor Jascinda Dharma Guest of HonourRtn.S. ChithambaranPillai, President, Rotary club of Nagercoil Town inaugurated the club in the college.
